# Decision Log — Kode With Greg PRD

## Session: 2026-05-20

### DL-1: Product Vision
**Decision:** Website's primary job is to spark a project idea and convert to a direct contact (email or call to Greg). Not informational, not a booking system.
**Source:** Greg, coaching session.

### DL-2: Contact Mechanism
**Decision:** CTA always resolves to email or call Greg directly. No form, no booking widget, no calendar integration.
**Source:** Greg, coaching session.

### DL-3: Project Archetypes
**Decision:** Four archetype categories for the Spark Section: business website, game, Raspberry Pi project, online store.
**Source:** Greg ("websites, pi program, selling stuff, games").

### DL-4: Three Personas
**Decision:** Three adult personas — Curious Hobbyist, Local Parent, Local Entrepreneur. Kids are not a direct website audience; parents book for kids.
**Source:** Greg confirmed all suggested personas.

### DL-5: Three Session Tracks
**Decision:** Sessions are goal-grouped into three tracks: Build a Business, Build Something, Play Around. Greg determines which track fits each student via conversation — the site presents tracks for self-identification only.
**Source:** Greg ("build a business, build something, play around").

### DL-6: No Real Student Stories at Launch
**Decision:** Spark Section uses archetype cards (not real student testimonials) at launch. Real stories will be swapped in as a v2 content update after first cohort. Section designed with slots to enable this without structural changes.
**Source:** Greg confirmed "starting from scratch."
**Party mode input:** John (PM) recommended designing for easy swap-in; adopted as FR-2 requirement.

### DL-7: Voice and Tone
**Decision:** Direct, confident, action-first. No hedging language. Verb-forward copy. No "might," "could," "perhaps."
**Source:** Greg ("no nonsense, no maybe type words, all do type words").

### DL-8: Visual Direction
**Decision:** "Italian and cool" — confident, clean, stylish, not fussy. Color palette and visual system delegated to UX designer. No brand assets exist yet.
**Source:** Greg.

### DL-9: Infrastructure
**Decision:** AWS hosting provisioned via Terraform. Assumed static site (S3 + CloudFront or equivalent); no backend required.
**Source:** Greg ("I will be going to AWS using Terraform").

### DL-10: Analytics
**Decision:** Google Analytics (GA4) integrated on all pages for page view tracking and referral source capture.
**Source:** Greg confirmed "yes" to analytics.

### DL-11: Facebook Link (Conditional)
**Decision:** Contact page includes a Facebook link only if a Kode With Greg Facebook page exists at launch. If not, element is omitted.
**Source:** Greg ("maybe I'll create a Facebook Kode With Greg page also").

### DL-12: MVP Pages
**Decision:** Three pages in MVP: Homepage, Services & Sessions, Contact. No gallery, blog, online courses, or booking system.
**Source:** Product brief (confirmed during PRD coaching).
